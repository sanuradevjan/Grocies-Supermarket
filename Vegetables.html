Our Farms

Badulla

This farm is well-known for producing a variety of crops, including onions, with success. Part of the 60 model farms established by the Sarubima program, it unites Sinhalese-Tamil growers toward a single goal.

Bandarawela

In the company's supply chain, this is where the Grocies Vegetable Collecting Center is located. Additionally to boosting the local economy, it guarantees farmers' regular payments.

Nuwara Eliya

This farm is a component of Grocies' Sarubima initiative, which has replaced traditional agricultural practices with cutting-edge techniques to boost yields and cut waste. Out of the 60 model farms, 25 are located in Nuwara Eliya.
